WHAT ARE PANIC ATTACKS?

Sudden episode of intense fear or anxiety and physical symptoms, based on a perceived threat rather than imminent danger. Panic attacks are also a symptom of Post Traumatic Stress Disorder(PTSD).

HOW TO CONTROL YOUR PANIC ATTACKS

Self-treatment

First of all take a few deep breaths.

Now list 5 things you can see around you.

Now list 5 things you can hear.

Once that's done, list 5 things you are feeling physically(touch).

Now just take a few more deep breaths and relax.
